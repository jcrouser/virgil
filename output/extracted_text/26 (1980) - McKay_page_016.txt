VERGILIUS
Baldini Moscadi assesses Pharsalia 6 as a response to Aeneid 6. Scaeva is a
negative version of the pius Aeneas, and Lucan's book ends with a scene of
necromancy,
the antithesis of Vergil's underworld account. Baldwin finds
Vergilianisms in the Historia Augusta. Szelest treats Vergil as a source for
Lucan.
