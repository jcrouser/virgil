109.
Sir Roger Mynors, Vergil
Oxford, 1969.
The new Oxford University Classical Text replaces Sir Arthur Hirtzel's edition
with a revised text and the addition of an index nominum. The new apparatus
criticus provides a fuller account of the indirect tradition and incorporates the
evidence of ninth-century manuscripts.
