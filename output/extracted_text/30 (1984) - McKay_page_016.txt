Burns identifies distinctive elements in three Beneventan manuscripts containing Servius' commentary from the 8th, 10th, and late 10th or early 11th

XX and so expands on M. D. Reeve, 'The Textual Tradition of the Appendix
Vergiliana, II,' Maia 28 (1976) 233-54, and A. Rigg, "Medieval Latin Poetic
