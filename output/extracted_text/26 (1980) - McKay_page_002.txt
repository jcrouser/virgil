VERGILIAN BIBLIOGRAPHY
The present survey of Vergilian scholarship is the eighteenth in the Vergilius series. Classical
World 68:1
(1974), published in association with the
Vergilian Society,
Inc., contains my 'Recent Work on Vergil:
A
Bibliographical
Survey, 1964-73'. The survey has been reprinted in The Classical
World Bibliography of Vergil (Walter Donlan, ed.), Garland Publishing, Inc.
(New York & London, 1978). Subsequent surveys have appeared in Vergilius
19 (1973), 20 (1974), 21 (1975), 22 (1976), 23 (1977), 24 (1978) and 25
(1979).
Several noteworthy collections are contained inthe 1979-80 Bibliography.
One, edited by Raymond Chevallier, and entitled Présence de Virgile: Actes
du Colloque des 9, 11, 12 Décembre, 1976 (Paris 1978), appears repeatedly
and is abbreviated throughout as Présence. A. J.Boyles' thematic number of
Ramus 8:1 (1979) is entitled Vergil's Ascraean Song: Ramus Essays on the
Georgics; it appears repeatedly and is abbreviated throughout as Virgil's Ascraean Song. Four noteworthy bibliographies are awaiting publication inthe
already monumental ANRW: 'A Bibliography of Virgil's Eclogues' by Ward
W.
Briggs,
Jr.; and three additional bibliographies, all compiled by Werner
Suerbaum (Munich): 'Eine systematische Arbeitsbibliographie mit besonderer
Berücksichtigung der Aeneis'' 'Spezialbibliographie zu Vergils Geórgica '; and
'Von der Vita
Vergiliana
über die Accessus Vergiliani zum Zauberer Vergilianus: Probleme - Perspektiven - Analysen'. The ANRW bibliographies differin their chronological
limits:
Briggs continues his bibliography of the
Eclogues to 1977; Suerbaum closes his listings with 1975. Classical World will
publish my cumulative 'Recent Work on Vergil, 1974-1982' during the Bimillenium in1982.
Off prints of articles and bibliographic notices for inclusion in the annual
Vergilius review are always welcomed by the editor. Address correspondence
to Professor A. G. McKay, Department of Classics, McMaster University,
Hamilton, Ontario, Canada L8S 4M2.
