VERGILIAN BIBLIOGRAPHY 1985-86
In Memoriam - Robert Deryck Williams (1917-1986)
The present survey of Vergilian scholarship is the twenty-fourth
VERGILIUS series. Classical World 68:1 (1974), published in association with The
Vergilian Society, Inc., contains my "Recent Work on Vergil: A Bibliographical
Survey, 1964-73." The survey was reprinted in The Classical World Bibliography of
Vergil (Walter Donlan, ed.), Garland Publishing, Inc., (New York & London 1978).
Subsequent reviews of scholarship have appeared in Vergilius 19 (1973), 20 (1974),
21 (1975), 22 (1976), 23 (1977), 24 (1978), 25 (1979), 26 (1980), 2'/ (1981), 28
(1982), 29 (1983), 30 (1984), and 31 (1985).
This compilation owes much to the assistance and cooperation of Mills
Memorial Library of McMaster University, the Robarts Library of the University of
Toronto, and the Library of the Union Académique Internationale, Brussels,
Belgium. Offprints of articles and titles for inclusion in the annual VERGILIUS
bibliography were generously provided by readers, and scholars are once again
invited to send material and suggested entries to the editor of Vergilian Bibliography, c/o Dept. of Classics, McMaster University, Hamilton, Ontario, Canada L8S
4M2.
