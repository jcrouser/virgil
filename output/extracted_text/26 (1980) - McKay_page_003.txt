Paul Alpers' verse translation of the Eclogues is everything one might expect from a Professor of English, contemporary and lucid, more attractive
than Boyles' recent version (Melbourne 1976). The Delille reprint is artistically attractive. Eggenberger's analysis of the Vergilius Romanus miniatures is
iconographical and stylistic; the script is dated between A.D. 590 and 610.
Joly retraces well-worn paths with
useful comments. Lebek examines Aen.
1.683ff. ina discussion of epigrammatic lines at Pompeii.
