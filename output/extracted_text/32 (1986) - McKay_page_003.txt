The bimillenary fever has abated somewhat but the fall-out from 1981 and
1982 continues to appear in belated publications or delayed arrivals. Antonie
Wlosok's collected titles and notices is exemplary and enormously useful for those
who wish to assess recent trends in Vergilian scholarship and to discover centers of
concentration.
