and expression is most significantly evidenced. Pridik (34) has composed
a valuable study of structure which merits the closest attention from
scholars. Saint-Denis (36) uses Columella to explicate some Vergilian
cruces in G. 1 (56), 2 (9-37, and 3 (40-42). K.D. White (37), noted for
